1. Algorithm Validation & CorrectionThe Paper's Equation: The closed-form solution (Eq 16a) has a theoretical flaw. It treats the depth at $t-1$ ($d$) as a known constant. For objects moving longitudinally (away/toward the camera), this approximation fails, introducing systematic error even with perfect sensors.The Fix: We derived an Exact Corrected Solver by treating depth as a function of the unknown velocity ($d(\mathbf{t}) = d_{static} - \mathbf{k} \cdot \mathbf{t}$). This rearranges the linear algebra into a form $(M - C \otimes k) \cdot \mathbf{t} = B$, which solves for the velocity perfectly without approximation.2.
